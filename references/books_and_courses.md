# Books & Courses — Curated Reading List

Personal notes on each resource. Not a list of "what I read" but "what I learned from each."

## Mathematics & Foundations
- **3Blue1Brown** — *Essence of Linear Algebra*, *Essence of Calculus* (YouTube)
- **Deisenroth, Faisal, Ong** — *Mathematics for Machine Learning* (free PDF)
- **VanderPlas** — *Python Data Science Handbook* (free online)

## Machine Learning
- **Andrew Ng** — *Machine Learning Specialization* (Coursera)
- **Géron** — *Hands-On Machine Learning with Scikit-Learn, Keras & TensorFlow*
- **StatQuest** (Josh Starmer) — YouTube channel for visual ML/stats

## Deep Learning
- **Karpathy** — *Neural Networks: Zero to Hero* (YouTube series)
- **fast.ai** — *Practical Deep Learning for Coders*
- **d2l.ai** — *Dive into Deep Learning* (free, with code)
- **Andrew Ng** — *Deep Learning Specialization* (Coursera)
- **Sutton & Barto** — *Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction* (free online draft)

## NLP & Transformers
- **Vaswani et al.** — *Attention Is All You Need* (2017 paper)
- **Alammar** — *The Illustrated Transformer* (blog)
- **Karpathy** — *Let's build GPT* (YouTube)
- **Jurafsky & Martin** — *Speech and Language Processing* (free, chapters on attention)
- **Stanford CS224N** — Natural Language Processing with Deep Learning

## LLM Engineering
- **Chip Huyen** — *AI Engineering* (O'Reilly, 2025) + `chiphuyen/aie-book`
- **Alammar & Grootendorst** — *Hands-On Large Language Models*
- **Iusztin & Labonne** — *LLM Engineering Handbook*
- **Hugging Face** — *NLP/LLM Course*
- **Rafailov et al.** — *DPO: Direct Preference Optimization* (2023 paper)

## Production & MLOps
- **Chip Huyen** — *Designing Machine Learning Systems* (O'Reilly)
- **Goku Mohandas** — *Made With ML* (free, MLOps end-to-end)
- **OWASP** — *Top 10 for LLM Applications*

## Capstone Reference
- **Raschka** — *Build a Large Language Model (From Scratch)* (Manning)
- **Karpathy** — `nanoGPT`, `minGPT`, `llm.c` (GitHub repos)
