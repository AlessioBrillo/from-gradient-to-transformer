# Convex Optimization

**Authors**: Stephen Boyd, Lieven Vandenberghe
**Year**: 2004
**Publisher**: Cambridge University Press
**URL**: https://web.stanford.edu/~boyd/cvxbook/

**Used in Phase 1 for**: Convex sets and functions (Ch. 2–3), duality and KKT (Ch. 5)

**Key sections for ML foundations**:
- Ch. 2: Convex sets — definitions, examples, operations preserving convexity
- Ch. 3: Convex functions — first-order and second-order conditions, examples (quadratics, norms, log-sum-exp)
- Ch. 5: Duality — Lagrangian, KKT conditions, complementary slackness

**Why it matters**: The gold-standard reference for convex optimization theory. The first 5 chapters are accessible with undergraduate linear algebra and provide the theoretical guarantees that underpin logistic regression, linear SVM, and Lasso convergence.
