# Mathematics for Machine Learning

**Authors**: Marc Peter Deisenroth, A. Aldo Faisal, Cheng Soon Ong
**Year**: 2020
**URL**: https://mml-book.github.io/

**Used in Phase 1 for**: Linear algebra (Ch. 2-4), vector calculus (Ch. 5), probability and distributions (Ch. 6), optimization (Ch. 7), when?

**Key chapters for foundations**:
- Ch. 2: Linear Algebra — vectors, matrices, span, basis, norms
- Ch. 4: Matrix Decompositions — eigenvalues, SVD, Cholesky
- Ch. 5: Vector Calculus — gradients, Jacobians, Hessians, chain rule
- Ch. 6: Probability and Distributions — MLE, Bayesian inference
- Ch. 7: Optimization — convexity, Lagrange multipliers, KKT

**Why it matters**: Bridges the gap between pure math textbooks and ML papers. Every concept is motivated by a ML use case, not just abstract theory.
